# DMV Queen of Clean — AI Chatbot Content Questionnaire

This document contains all the questions we need answered to power your AI chat assistant. The AI will use your answers to respond to visitors on your website — answering questions about your services, pricing, availability, and whether you're the right fit for them.

**How to fill this out:**
- Answer in plain conversational language — write it the way you'd say it to a customer
- The more detail you give, the better the AI performs
- Skip anything that doesn't apply to your business
- Nothing here is public-facing until you approve it
